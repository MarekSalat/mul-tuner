Simple html5 guitar tuner

            
Plug in your guitar or mic and try to tune your instrument.

            
How to use

            

                Strum the string you want to tune little bit harder than you usually play.
                Use guitars tuners to tune string. When you see your desired note try to get dot to the blue range.
                If the dot is in the blue range long enough your note is accepted by a green pulsing circle.
                
    
                    
  1. Red dot means your note too low
    2. 
                    
  2. Green dot means your note too high

  3. Blue dot means your note okay

  5. Green pulsing indicates good tuning for your string

Tips and limitations

            
Due technical difficulties you can achieve best results if you strum first or second string at 12 fret.

            
If you are using mic, try to fiddle setting. My recommendations are gamma=0.98, min decibels=-65, fft smoothing=0.9)

            
* Low A (~110Hz) string might be challenging to tune.
                Best practise is to down tune the string just a little approximately -6 cents to get right tone.
                Tune bias might be added in upcoming feature update.
